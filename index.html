Why this matters to neighbors.

Parking gets tighter

More units and fewer spaces than the zoning table lists as required means more pressure on a neighborhood already short on parking.

Congestion gets worse

More cars, deliveries, ride-share pickups, moving trucks, garbage pickup, and garage activity all land on the same tight streets.

Safety gets harder

Near-zero setbacks affect visibility, pedestrian comfort, and practical emergency access around the site.

Helping now creates leverage.

An appeal can push the approval back for serious review, create pressure for a more responsible plan, and show that Boulevard East residents are not asleep at the switch.
