What neighbors will feel.

More street conflict

More garage trips, deliveries, ride-share stops, and double-parking mean more traffic conflict on an already tight block.

Parking pressure

The zoning table lists 128 parking stalls. The approved proposal has 80. Missing parking does not disappear. It moves onto the block.

Emergency access

With the building pushed almost to the edges of the site, fire access and ladder access become harder.

Permanent canyon effect

A seven-story wall means less sky, less light, and a darker street. The court record already notes summer shadows reaching Versailles' pool after about 4:45 PM.

Heat and runoff

With 96% impervious coverage, almost no soft surface is left. More hard surface means more stored heat and more stormwater stress.

A bad precedent

Once one medium-density site gets treated like high density, the next developer asks for the same treatment.
